Achievements

The three phases of tactile exploration

The perception of a texture or material is not homogeneous. We show that the onset of touch can also have a role in the tactile perception of smooth materials. [read more]

The perception of changes in friction

We mounted a high-precision ultrasonic tactile displays capable of reducing friction on a robotic platform especially designed to deliver controlled passive slides on the unmoving finegrtip. We could then measure the human eprception of chanegs in friction. [read more]
